About Us

The Bridge’ is a concept based on book rolling that enables enthusiastic readers to bring in unused or read books from home to swap or donate. Moreover, we have also donation services for donating goods such as clothing, toys, food or vehicles. We have initiated this project o encourage more readers and help the needy ones as well. The sole purpose of ‘The Bridge’ is to act as a bridge between the enthusiastic readers and guide them to a destination where the reading of books and donating things will be made easily accessible. Furthermore, to specifically encourage them.
